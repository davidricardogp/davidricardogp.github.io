Bio

I am an economist from Peru, currently based in Barcelona, Spain. I work as a Research Assistant at IESE Business School’s Entrepreneurship & Innovation Center.

My academic and professional interests lie at the intersection of public policy, innovation and entrepreneurship. I am especially interested in how governments, universities, corporations, and investors can support technological entrepreneurship and help startups overcome financing, procurement, and ecosystem barriers.

Before joining IESE, I worked on building and raising funds for a startup, as well as in economic consulting, econometrics academic research, and teaching assistance. My long-term goal is to pursue graduate and doctoral studies and contribute to research that helps improve public policies on innovation.
