David Ricardo Gonzales Peña

Economist interested in public policy, innovation, entrepreneurship, and economic development.

I am an economist from Peru, currently based in Barcelona, Spain. I work as a Research Assistant at IESE Business School’s Entrepreneurship & Innovation Center, where I contribute to applied research on policymaking, corporate innovation, technology transfer, startups, and scaleups.

My academic and professional interests lie at the intersection of innovation policy, entrepreneurship ecosystems, public-private collaboration, and evidence-based institutional design. I am especially interested in how governments, universities, corporations, and investors can support technological entrepreneurship and help startups and scaleups overcome financing, procurement, and ecosystem barriers.

Before joining IESE, I worked in startup fundraising, economic consulting, academic research, and teaching assistance. My long-term goal is to pursue graduate and doctoral studies and contribute to rigorous research that helps improve public policy and economic opportunity.
