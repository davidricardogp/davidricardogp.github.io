Bio

I am an economist from Peru, currently based in Barcelona, Spain. I work as a Research Assistant at IESE Business School’s Entrepreneurship & Innovation Center.

My work focuses on applied research at the intersection of entrepreneurship, innovation policy and public-private collaboration.

Before joining IESE, I worked on building and raising funds for a startup, as well as in economic consulting, econometrics academic research, and teaching assistance. My long-term goal is to pursue doctoral studies and do research.
